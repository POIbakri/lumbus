**Lumbus - Stay Connected Anywhere**

Lumbus is a global eSIM provider that makes staying connected while traveling effortless. We offer instant mobile data plans for over 150 countries, delivered directly to your phone in seconds - no physical SIM card required.

**How It Works:**
1. Choose a data plan for your destination
2. Receive your eSIM instantly via email
3. Tap to install automatically or scan the QR code
4. Connect when you land - your plan activates on first use

**Why Travelers Choose Lumbus:**
- **Instant Delivery** - Get your eSIM in seconds, not days
- **150+ Countries** - One app for worldwide connectivity
- **Save Up to 90%** - Avoid expensive roaming charges
- **Keep Your Number** - Use eSIM for data while your regular SIM handles calls
- **2-Minute Setup** - Install before you even board your flight
- **Hotspot Included** - Share your data with other devices
- **No Contracts** - Pay only for what you need

Lumbus eliminates the hassle of hunting for local SIM cards at airports, dealing with language barriers, or paying outrageous roaming fees. Whether you're a frequent business traveler, digital nomad, or vacation adventurer, Lumbus keeps you connected to what matters most.
